THE STRANGER THINGS

Stranger Things is set in the fictional rural town of Hawkins, Indiana, during the 1980s. The Hawkins National Laboratory secretly does experiments into the paranormal and supernatural. Inadvertently, they have created a portal to an alternate dimension, "the Upside Down".
